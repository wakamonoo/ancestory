How to Use These Colors:
Background:
Use Peachy Beige (#F1D1B5) for your main background to keep things light and warm.

Primary Buttons:
Apply Golden Yellow (#FFB84D) for primary buttons like "Login" or "Settings," which will stand out and feel welcoming.

Accent Elements (Links, Navigation):
Use Warm Coral (#FF6F61) for call-to-action buttons, navigation highlights, or any elements that you want to draw attention to.

Hover States & Secondary Buttons:
Rich Salmon (#FF9A8B) can be used for hover effects or secondary buttons to add contrast without making the UI too harsh.
